MALL ROAD

Mall Road is the best location in Murree. It is the most famous street in Murree. It is the central shopping area in Murree. There are lots of favorite hotels and restaurants on this street. Experience the stunning beauty of Murree’s lush mountains and beautiful valleys with a tour of Mall Road. There are many tourist spots and beautiful places to visit on Mall Road. The Murree Botanical Garden is one of Murree’s most popular tourist attractions. There are also various shops and eateries on the street. The first part of the road is dominated by several shops selling souvenirs, while eateries dominate the second part of the road. Visitors can enjoy several activities while visiting Mall Road. Visitors can also see some beautiful houses and mosques on the road. If you are looking for a day trip, Murree is the perfect destination.
