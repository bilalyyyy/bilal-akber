BHURBAN

Bhurban is a tourist destination with unique flora, and a fauna with a variety species not found elsewhere in Pakistan.[citation needed] It is known for the hiking trails in the nearby Ayubia National Park. The '5 star' Pearl Continental Hotel - Mount Pleasant Apartments is one of several resorts in Bhurban that serve tourists visiting the Murree Hills and the national park.
